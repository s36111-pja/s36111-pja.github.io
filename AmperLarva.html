Amper Larvas are known as parasites that with just their presence can create a massive power outages all around the cities. Instead of targeting lifefroms, they live on draining energy from power boxes, electricity-powered vehicles and other large sources of power.

Appearance

These larvas are large in size, being around 20 centimeters tall. They usually stand out due to the dirty yellowish colors and light blue lines on top of their bodies. Though they possess a pair of eyes, their sight is very weak.

Behavior

Their brain, as for a bug, is not very well developed. For the most of their life they quietly roam around and search for any signs of electricity. Once they stick to the source, they start laying eggs on that spot to ensure a healthy hatching and to drain energy for themselves.

Past

The only information about this parasite is that they evolved from a different species that went extinct a few decades ago.

Variants and Related Lifeforms

All related lifeforms for amper larvas went extinct.
