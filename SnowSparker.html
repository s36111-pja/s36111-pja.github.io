Snow Sparkers adapted into glacial enviorment by using their electricity as a heat source. This choice resulted in losing the ability of utilizing electricity to defend themselves from the threats. Unlike Sparkers, this kind's fur brightened significantly to blend into snowy terrains

Appearance

Snow Sparkers possess a white fur and developed their spine to stand properly compared to normal Sparkers. The electricity inside this fauna warm up their entire bodies with an exception of the top of the head, in which three thick icicles appear way before they reach adulthood. Snow Sparkers additionally start utilizing their tail, by dipping it in water until a small chunk of ice appears.

Behavior

They're way more relaxed compared to their normal counterpart, but still can get mildly agressive when someone comes too close. They usually travel in groups of five to gather food and other resources to survive. Snow Sparkers also fully left cave areas and started to live in small notches found near glacial cliffs.

Past

Ever since the The Great Release - an event 50 years in which rebelious groups released over five thousand enslaved animals all over the cities from the hands of the goverments that used them for wealth, some Sparkers ran away up north into the snowy terrains in which they swiftly adapted to the new dangers. Legends say an unknown divinity granted them a blessing to withstand such enviorment. People nowdays consider these creatures as one of the blessed ones and some religions say killing one will bring the unknown's god wrath upon the killer.
