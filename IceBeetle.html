Ice Beetle are creatures only found deep inside glacial caverns near a Frozen Heart. It is not confirmed whether they protect the organ or feed on it.

Appearance

One of the larger bugs found in nature. They use their tough horn in front of their heads to charge into a target and slowly freeze them to death. They also posses two pairs of wings that they use all the time and three pair of small limbs that are bound to their bodies. A large sting at their bottom is used to keep the victim in place.

Behavior

Ice beetles never leave their caverns, even if their lives are in danger. They always attack in group and use vibrations to communicate and select the target with other beetles.

Past

The past about Ice Beetles is still unknown. Required futher reasearch.

Variants and Related Lifeforms

Frozen Heart is a lifeform Ice Beetle spend their entire lives around. The purpose of that is still unknown.
