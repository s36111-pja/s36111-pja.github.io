Hoarses are one of the most unique faunas found in very dry climates. Most of their muscles, even though they don't possess many, are found near the jaw area. They can be compared to a walking zombie, since they don't need to consume much food or water.

Appearance

Hoarse's skin is very similar to a standard cloth, which is missing in various parts around their body. Most common places in which their skin is missing is their rear where from one to three bones stick out, the area of a stomach and sides of their limbs. Though they look very vulnerable, Their sharp jaw makes them one of the most dangerous fauna in the desert areas./p>

Behavior

These creatures are not very agressive. They feed on cactuses and the greenery found in oasis areas. Yet, it doesn't mean're in any way harmless. When a prey comes close enough to a Hoarse, they'll instantly attack, which most of the time results in a bloody execution. Their jaw is strong enough to rip off a lib from the most damgerous predators.

Past

Reasearch shows that they once stood on two feet and were acting much more agressive. There's also a myth that says Hoarses look like this nowdays due to a large epidemy that was present around 500 years ago.
