Frozen Heart is a lifeform of an unknown origin found in the glacial caverns near the Mt.Relazan. The caverns surrounding the heart are the home of Ice Beetles

Appearance

Legends tell its a giant crimson organ covered with reinforced ice. Any researches that apparently found the heart either got their mind corrupted or got devoured by local ice beetles.

Behavior

The heart alone does not have a specific behavior, but its known to corrupt the mind of those who saw the organism and erase large part of their memories. The corruption is more effective on those with weak minds, since some of the corrupted researchers were in way worse condition than the others.
