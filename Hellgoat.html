Hellgoats are the most aggressive creatures found on the peaks of the mountains. Even a minor disturbance can result in a massive rampage. Name "hellgoat" was given to this creature by a known biologist and adventurer Gregory Lafind before he got fatally massacred by one just a week after.

Appearance

Hellgoats naturally possess a white fur, but due to their chaotic personality, they're usually visible with a dirty grey colored fur. They are equipped with a pair of long, spiked horns and three tails that during creature's rampage act on their own.

Behavior

They're very chaotic and any sudden sound can make them go mad. Their recklessness can explain their weird behavior that is present when this fauna is spotted near steep cliffs. Jump Duel, the activity they do during their presence in there, is about a very high leaps two of the hellgoats make usually above large canyons or cliffs to determine who should be the leader of the group. Both competitors try to hit eachother mid-air and knock them out into the gap. It often results in both of them falling in there.

Past

This fauna is said to live in the mountain areas for ages. It's still a mystery how in the world they survived this long with their behavior like this.

Variants and Related Lifeforms

Many creatures have a similar body structure to Hellgoats, but none of them were officially confirmed.
